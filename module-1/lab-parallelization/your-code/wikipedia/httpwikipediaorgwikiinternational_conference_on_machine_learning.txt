<b>International Conference on Machine Learning</b> (<b>ICML</b>) is the leading international <a href="/wiki/Academic_conference" title="Academic conference">academic conference</a> in <a href="/wiki/Machine_learning" title="Machine learning">machine learning</a>. Along with <a href="/wiki/Conference_on_Neural_Information_Processing_Systems" title="Conference on Neural Information Processing Systems">NeurIPS</a> and <a href="/wiki/International_Conference_on_Learning_Representations" title="International Conference on Learning Representations">ICLR</a>, it is one of the three primary conferences of high impact in <a href="/wiki/Machine_learning" title="Machine learning">machine learning</a> and <a href="/wiki/Artificial_intelligence" title="Artificial intelligence">artificial intelligence</a> research.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> It is supported by the <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.machinelearning.org/">International Machine Learning Society</a> (IMLS). Precise dates vary from year to year, but paper submissions are generally due at the end of January, and the conference is generally held during the following July. The first ICML was held 1980 in <a href="/wiki/Pittsburgh" title="Pittsburgh">Pittsburgh</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<div id="toc"
